MISFITS: NUCLEAR WASTELAND OPERATES AS A MEDIUM ROLEPLAY FALLOUT ENVIRONMENT.
THE GOAL IS NOT ONLY ENGAGING GAMEPLAY, BUT THE CREATION OF
MEMORABLE STORIES SHAPED BY PLAYER CHOICE.

THE WASTELAND IS HARSH AND CONSEQUENCES MATTER.
YOUR SURVIVAL DEPENDS ON YOUR ACTIONS, YOUR ALLIANCES, AND YOUR JUDGMENT.

ADMINISTRATIVE INTERVENTION IS RARE AND RESERVED
FOR RULE ENFORCEMENT, NOT STORY RESCUE.
======================================
DIRECTIVE 0 — CONDUCT
THIS IS A COLLABORATIVE ENVIRONMENT.

TOXICITY, EGO PLAY, AND BEHAVIOR DESIGNED TO RUIN OTHERS' ENJOYMENT ARE NOT TOLERATED.
  • PLAY TO CREATE STORY, NOT TO "WIN"
  • RESPECT ADMINISTRATORS AND DEVELOPERS
  • COOPERATE DURING ADMIN INTERACTIONS
  • DO NOT DISCONNECT DURING ACTIVE TICKETS
  • REPORT TECHNICAL ISSUES PROMPTLY
⚠ ADMINISTRATION MAY INTERVENE IN SITUATIONS NOT EXPLICITLY COVERED IF BEHAVIOR DAMAGES THE COMMUNITY OR THE ROUND.
PUNISHMENTS ARE ISSUED AT STAFF DISCRETION BASED ON SEVERITY, CONTEXT, AND HISTORY.
======================================
DIRECTIVE 1 — GRIEFING
GRIEFING IS ANY ACTION TAKEN PRIMARILY TO DISRUPT GAMEPLAY OR DEGRADE THE EXPERIENCE OF OTHERS WITHOUT MEANINGFUL IN-CHARACTER REASONING.
Examples Include
  • DELIBERATE DESTRUCTION WITH NO RP JUSTIFICATION
  • TRAP PLACEMENT INTENDED ONLY TO FRUSTRATE
  • LARGE-SCALE DISRUPTION WITHOUT NARRATIVE BASIS
⚠ LACK OF INTENT DOES NOT ALWAYS EXCUSE LARGE-SCALE DAMAGE.
IF YOUR ACTIONS SEVERELY IMPACT THE ROUND WITHOUT REASONABLE RP LOGIC, THEY MAY BE TREATED AS GRIEFING.
======================================
DIRECTIVE 2 — FAIR PLAY
2.1 — Metagaming
USING OUT-OF-CHARACTER KNOWLEDGE TO INFLUENCE IN-CHARACTER ACTIONS IS PROHIBITED.
PROHIBITED INFORMATION SOURCES
  • DISCORD
  • STREAMS OR GHOSTING
  • OOC CHAT
  • EXTERNAL COMMUNICATION
MAY NOT BE USED IN CHARACTER.
Streaming is allowed, but responsibility for information leaks lies with the streamer.
2.2 — Multiaccounting
  • MULTIPLE ACCOUNTS IN THE SAME ROUND ARE FORBIDDEN
  • BAN EVASION IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED
  • ALTERNATE ACCOUNTS TO AVOID PUNISHMENT HISTORY ARE NOT ALLOWED
⚠ VIOLATIONS MAY RESULT IN PERMANENT BANS.
2.3 — Exploits
  • NO CHEATS, MACROS, OR THIRD-PARTY TOOLS
  • NO INTENTIONAL BUG ABUSE
  • NO DELIBERATE SERVER STRAIN
REPORT DISCOVERED BUGS THROUGH OFFICIAL CHANNELS.

DIRECTIVE 3 — ROLEPLAY STANDARDS
CHARACTERS MUST FIT THE FALLOUT SETTING IN TONE, BEHAVIOR, AND NAMING.
3.1 — Character Identity
  • NO MEME NAMES OR REAL-WORLD REFERENCES
  • NO REUSE OF DEAD OR PERMANENTLY IMPRISONED CHARACTERS WITHOUT APPROVAL
  • ADMINISTRATION MAY REQUIRE NAME CHANGES
3.2 — Communication
  • DO NOT BREAK IMMERSION WITH MECHANICAL TERMS
  • KEEP OOC TOPICS OUT OF IC CHANNELS
  • OOC CHANNELS MUST REMAIN RESPECTFUL
3.3 — Atmosphere
  • DO NOT REFERENCE ADMINS OR GAME SYSTEMS IN CHARACTER
  • PROVIDE IC REASONS FOR ABSENCE WHEN POSSIBLE
3.4 — Knowledge Limits
YOUR CHARACTER IS NOT ALL-KNOWING.
DO NOT REFERENCE TECHNOLOGY OR KNOWLEDGE YOUR CHARACTER WOULD NOT REALISTICALLY POSSESS.

DIRECTIVE 6 — ARMED CONFLICT
6.1 — Escalation
DIALOGUE MUST PRECEDE LETHAL FORCE.
REQUIREMENTS
  • FACE-TO-FACE ESCALATION
  • CLEAR DEMAND GIVEN
  • TIME TO RESPOND
EXCEPTIONS
  • ACTIVE FIREFIGHTS
  • IMMEDIATE THREATS
  • WILDLIFE
  • FACTION WARFARE
6.2 — Robbery
  • DO NOT REMOVE ALL SURVIVAL ITEMS
  • NON-RESISTING VICTIMS MUST LIVE
  • RESISTANCE SHIFTS RESPONSIBILITY
6.3 — Faction Warfare
  • FACTIONS BEGIN NEUTRAL
  • ONLY LEADERS MAY DECLARE WAR
  • WAR BEGINS FIVE MINUTES AFTER FACE-TO-FACE DECLARATION
⚠ KILLING DIPLOMATS OR SURRENDERED INDIVIDUALS IS FORBIDDEN.
6.4 — Early Raids
FACTION BASES MAY NOT BE RAIDED WITHIN THE FIRST 30 MINUTES.
6.5 — Faction Equipment & Impersonation
ALL FACTIONS MAY BE LOOTED OF ARMOR, UNIFORMS, WEAPONS, AND ITEMS.
  • ANY PERSON IMPERSONATING A FACTION MAY BE ESCALATED UPON
  • WEARING A FACTION'S ARMOR GIVES THAT FACTION PERMISSION TO ESCALATE
6.6 — Faction Raids
ALL FACTION RAIDS MUST BE AHELPED FOR APPROVAL BEFORE COMMENCING.
IF DENIED OR NO RESPONSE IS GIVEN — THE ATTACKING PARTY MUST LEAVE.
6.7 — Conflict Intervention
PLAYERS WHO CLEARLY SURRENDER MAY NOT BE EXECUTED IF NO CROSSFIRE EXISTS.

FACTION OR GANG MEMBERS WITH IDENTICAL UNIFORMS MAY ASSIST ALLIES IN ACTIVE CONFLICT.
======================================
DIRECTIVE 7 — NEW LIFE RULE
  • PLAYERS MAY NOT REJOIN ON THE SAME CHARACTER TO BYPASS DEATH
  • REVIVED CHARACTERS FORGET THE CIRCUMSTANCES OF THEIR DEATH
  • KNOWLEDGE FROM PREVIOUS ROUNDS MAY NOT BE USED FOR ADVANTAGE
======================================
DIRECTIVE 8 — FEAR ROLEPLAY
FEARRP APPLIES WHEN A PLAYER IS REVIVED IN A SECURE LOCATION.
Example Scenario
LEGION KILLS A BOS PALADIN AND REVIVES THEM AT THEIR CAMP.
THE PALADIN MUST NOW VALUE THEIR LIFE AND FOLLOW FEARRP.
  • ESCAPE ATTEMPTS REQUIRE ADMIN APPROVAL VIA AHELP
  • FAILED ATTEMPTS REQUIRE NEW APPROVAL
