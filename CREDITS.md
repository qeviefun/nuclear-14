## SS14 Fork Lineage

### Nuclear 14 (Vault Overseers)
- **Repository:** [Vault-Overseers/nuclear-14](https://github.com/Vault-Overseers/nuclear-14)
- The original Fallout-themed SS14 fork. Primary source of Fallout content, tile sets, lobby screens, and entity data ported into this project.

### Nuclear 14 (Forge Station / Corvax)
- **Repository:** [Forge-Station/nuclear-14](https://github.com/Forge-Station/nuclear-14)

## SS13 (BYOND) Codebase Credits

Assets, sprites, and audio ported or adapted from the following SS13 codebases:

### Big Iron (F13 / SS13)
- **Repository:** [Foundation-19/Big-Iron](https://github.com/Foundation-19/Big-Iron)
- **Used for:** Creature/NPC audio samples including Bloatfly, Cazador, Centaur, Deathclaw, Dog, Eyebot, Gecko, Ghoul, Gutsy, Handy, Molerat, Nightstalker, Protectron, Radscorpion, Roach, Scorpion, Sentry, Supermutant sounds.
- Fallout 13 SS13 codebase.

### Desert Rose 2 (F13 / SS13)
- **Repository:** [DesertRose2/desertrose](https://github.com/DesertRose2/desertrose)
- **Used for:** Creature audio (Assaultron sounds, credit: Tron-117); machine audio (grill ignition and steam effects); terrain tile sprites (ground.dmi, dirt and rubble tiles ported and modified by Peptide90).

### Lone Star (F13 / SS13)
- **Repository:** [LoneStarF13/LoneStar](https://github.com/LoneStarF13/LoneStar)
- **Used for:** Consumable food sprites (committed by FantasticFwoosh).

### Fortuna (SS13)
- **Repository:** [FortunaSS13/Fortuna](https://github.com/FortunaSS13/Fortuna) (commit `2a9408a47e2f83d945335e4feeeeafb552173e6f`)
- **Used for:** Tile sprites (grass, dirt, concrete road, and various floor tiles), edited for SS14 by Peptide; further tweaked by Zeta_Null.

### Official-Outcast13 / Fallout-13
- **Repository:** [Official-Outcast13/Fallout-13](https://github.com/Official-Outcast13/Fallout-13)
- **Used for:** Select Fallout-themed audio assets.
